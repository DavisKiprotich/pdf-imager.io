Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

1. Data Collection

PDF Imager is designed with privacy as a priority. We do not collect, store, or sell your personal data. All files processed (Images, PDFs, Word docs) remain on your device unless sent to our processing partner, CloudConvert, for conversion.

2. Third-Party Services

For complex conversions (PDF to Word, Word to PDF), we use CloudConvert. Files are temporarily uploaded to their secure servers, processed, and then deleted immediately after conversion. No permanent copy is kept.

3. Local Storage

Your conversion history and files are stored strictly on your local device. Deleting the app will remove all associated data permanently.

4. Subscriptions

Subscription data is managed entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not have access to your payment information.
